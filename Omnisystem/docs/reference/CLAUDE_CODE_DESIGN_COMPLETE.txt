🔐 SECURITY & APPROVAL
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Risk Scoring:

Low Risk (auto-approve):
  • read_file, @-mention, model inference

Medium Risk (quick modal):
  • file_write (small, non-sensitive)
  • git_commit

High Risk (detailed review):
  • file_delete, git_force_push

Critical (typed confirmation):
  • write to .env, *.key, *.pem
  • exec with rm -rf, dd
